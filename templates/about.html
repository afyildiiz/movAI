WHAT IS MOVAI?

MovAI is your AI-powered personal assistant for all things movies and TV shows. Engage in a conversation to get movie and TV show recommendations, detailed information about any title you're curious about, and even lists of popular or top-rated movies by genre.
Powered by The Movie Database (TMDb) API, MovAI taps into a vast database of movies and TV shows, providing you with the most up-to-date information. When you ask a question or make a request, MovAI uses the TMDb API to search for relevant data and presents it to you in an easy-to-understand format.

FEATURES

  • Movie & TV Show Recommendations: Tell MovAI the genre, actor, or director you're interested in, and it will suggest great options for you to watch.
  • Detailed Information: Ask MovAI about any movie or TV show you're curious about. Get instant access to information like title, overview, release date, genres, production countries, a TMDb link, and even where it's streaming.
  • Popular & Top-Rated Lists: Ask MovAI for lists of currently popular movies or the best movies in a specific genre.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Chatting with MovAI is easy and fun. Ask your questions in natural language and get instant answers.
